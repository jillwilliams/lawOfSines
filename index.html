The Law of Sines

The Law of Sines is used to Solve a Triangle. It can be used for any type of triangle.
Unlike SIN, COS, and TAN, that can only be applied to a Right Triangle, the Law of Sines is what you would want to use to Solve for the side lengths and angle measures of any triangle:

  • Acute Triangles
  • Obtuse Triangles
  • Scalene Triangles
  • Isosceles Triangles
  • Equilateral Triangles
  • and Equiangular Triangles

When using the formula, you will only use 2 out of the 3 fractions. The two you pick depends on the information you are given.
